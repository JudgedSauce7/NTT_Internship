Welcome to Document Cleaner !

Document images which are the input for key information extraction in multiple

business process often fail due to noise in images, which leads to poor OCR results.

Hence a deep learning model is implemented to process the images
and help in improving the performance of the whole process.

This can essentially remove water-marks, background noise, underline noise and skewness in the text.

Try it out by choosing a document and clicking the Process button
